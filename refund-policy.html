Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refund practices

Application Fee Refunds

At Aries College of Health Management & Technology, we understand that circumstances may change after submitting an application. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent for all applicants.

Eligible for Full Refund (100%)

  • Application Processing Error: If we are unable to process your application due to technical issues on our end
  • Duplicate Payment: If you accidentally make multiple payments for the same application
  • System Error: If payment is processed but application is not received due to system failure
  • Course Cancellation: If the program you applied for is cancelled by the institution

Eligible for Partial Refund (50%)

  • Withdrawal Before Processing: If you withdraw your application before we begin processing it (within 24 hours of submission)
  • Documentation Issues: If you cannot provide required documentation and choose to withdraw

Non-Refundable

  • Processing Started: Once we begin processing your application (after 24 hours)
  • Application Approved: If your application is approved and you choose not to enroll
  • Application Rejected: If your application is rejected due to incomplete information or failure to meet requirements
  • Change of Mind: If you simply change your mind about applying

Refund Process

  1. Request Submission: Submit a written refund request to admissions@ariescollege.edu.ng
  2. Review Period: We will review your request within 5-7 business days
  3. Approval Notification: You will receive an email notification if your refund is approved
  4. Processing Time: Approved refunds will be processed within 10-15 business days
  5. Payment Method: Refunds will be made to the original payment method used

Required Information for Refund Requests

  • Full name as it appears on the application
  • Application reference number
  • Email address used during application
  • Phone number
  • Reason for refund request
  • Date of application submission
  • Payment transaction ID (if available)

Important Notes

  • All refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of payment
  • Processing fees charged by payment gateways are non-refundable
  • Refund decisions are final and binding
  • This policy is subject to change with prior notice
  • For disputes, please contact our customer service team
