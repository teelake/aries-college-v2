About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to healthcare education

Our Mission

To create, preserve, and communicate knowledge in Health Science, Technology, Behavioural and Management Sciences, and contribute to the well-being of people in Oyo State, Nigeria, and globally.

We are committed to providing quality education that empowers our students to become competent healthcare professionals who can make meaningful contributions to the healthcare sector.

Our Vision

To be the most credible College of Health Technology, producing skilled, well-trained graduates ready to serve in healthcare systems with excellence.

We aspire to be a leading institution in healthcare education, recognized for our innovative programs, dedicated faculty, and successful graduates who excel in their chosen fields.

Our History

A journey of excellence in healthcare education

Foundation

Aries College of Health Management & Technology was established with a vision to provide quality healthcare education and training to students in Nigeria.

Program Development

We developed comprehensive programs in Health Information Management, Hospitality Management, and other healthcare-related fields to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals.

Accreditation

Our programs received proper accreditation and recognition from relevant educational and healthcare authorities, ensuring our graduates meet industry standards.

Growth & Expansion

Today, we continue to expand our programs and facilities, maintaining our commitment to excellence in healthcare education and producing graduates who make a difference.
