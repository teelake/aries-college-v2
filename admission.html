Admission Requirements

What you need to apply for our programs

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum of 5 credits in relevant subjects
  • Must include English and Mathematics
  • SSCE/WASSCE, NECO, or equivalent

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Academic certificates
  • Passport photograph
  • Application fee payment receipt

Application Process

  • Submit online application
  • Pay application fee
  • Submit required documents
  • Attend interview (if required)
  • Receive admission letter

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admission

What are the admission requirements for your programs?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, you need a minimum of 5 credits in relevant subjects including English and Mathematics. Please check our admission page for specific requirements for each program.

How long are the programs?

Our programs range from 2-3 years depending on the course. Health Information Management is 3 years, while most other programs are 2 years. Certificate programs are typically 2 years as well.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our website or visit our campus. The application process includes filling out forms, submitting required documents, and payment of application fees. Visit our Apply Now page for detailed instructions.

Do you offer financial assistance or scholarships?

When do classes start?

Classes typically start in March for the new academic session. Orientation programs are usually held a week before classes begin to help new students settle in.
